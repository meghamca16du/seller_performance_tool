On Time Delivery

This is the number of seller-fulfilled orders with shipments done after the expected shipment date divided by the total number of seller-fulfilled orders in the relevant time period. Orders that are shipped late may lead to a negative impact on customer experience.

Hit To Success Ratiopercentage

It is defined as the number of sales of a product divided by the number of customers who visited the site to find out about the product in the relevant time period. Greater the hit to success ratio, better the performance.

Return Rate percentage

It is calculated as the number of returned orders or products as a percentage of the number that has been sold. A high return rate is not a good score as it reflects consumer unhappiness.

Positive Feedbacks percentage

It the percentage of positive feedbacks, calculated as number of positive feedbacks divided by total number of feedbacks in the relevant time period.

Negative Feedbackspercentage

It the percentage of negative feedbacks, calculated as number of negative feedbacks divided by total number of feedbacks in the relevant time period. Performance is negatively affected when you receive 1-star or 2-star feedback comments.
