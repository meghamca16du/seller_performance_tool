Late Shipment

{{ trait_value.late_shipment }} This is the number of seller-fulfilled orders with shipments done after the expected shipment date divided by the total number of seller-fulfilled orders in the relevant time period. Orders that are shipped late may lead to a negative impact on customer experience.

On Time Delivery

This is the number of seller-fulfilled orders that are delivered earlier than or on the expected delivery date divided by the total number of seller-fulfilled orders in the relevant time period.
A high percentage of On Time Delivery results in a good customer experience.

Hit To Success Ratio

It is defined as the number of sales of a product divided by the number of customers who visited the site to find out about the product in the relevant time period.
Greater the hit to success ratio, better the performance.

Return Rate

It is calculated as the number of returned orders or products as a percentage of the number that has been sold.
A high return rate is not a good score as it reflects consumer unhappiness.

Positive Feedbacks

It the percentage of positive feedbacks, calculated as number of positive feedbacks divided by total number of feedbacks in the relevant time period.

Negative Feedbacks

It the percentage of negative feedbacks, calculated as number of negative feedbacks divided by total number of feedbacks in the relevant time period. Performance is negatively affected when you receive 1-star or 2-star feedback comments.
